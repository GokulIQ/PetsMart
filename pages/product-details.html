Product Information

Our Premium Dry Dog Food is crafted with real chicken as the first ingredient, delivering complete and balanced nutrition for adult dogs of all breeds. This grain-free formula supports healthy digestion, a shiny coat, and sustained energy throughout the day.

Developed in partnership with Royal Canin, each kibble is sized for optimal chewing and enriched with omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids for skin and coat health.

Chicken, chicken meal, sweet potatoes, peas, chicken fat (preserved with mixed tocopherols), flaxseed, natural flavour, salmon oil, dried chicory root, blueberries, cranberries, vitamins (A, D3, E, B12), minerals (zinc, iron, copper, manganese), and probiotics.

Guaranteed Analysis: Crude protein 28%, crude fat 16%, crude fibre 4%, moisture 10%.

Feed according to your dog's weight and activity level. Always provide fresh water.

  • Small dogs (5–10 kg): ½ to 1 cup daily
  • Medium dogs (10–25 kg): 1 to 2 cups daily
  • Large dogs (25–45 kg): 2 to 3½ cups daily

Transition gradually over 7–10 days when switching from another food. Consult your vet for puppies, seniors, or dogs with special dietary needs.

Delivery: Standard local delivery within 2–3 business days. Free on orders over $50. Same-day pickup available at our Petville store.

Returns: Unopened bags may be returned within 14 days with receipt for a full refund. Opened food cannot be returned for hygiene reasons — contact us if you experience quality issues.
