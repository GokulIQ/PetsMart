About PetsMart

Discover our story, meet the team behind the paw prints, and learn how we give back to the community we love.

Our Story

Born from a Passion for Animals

PetsMart started in 2010 when founders Maria and David opened a small corner shop with one simple belief: every pet deserves love, quality care, and a place they can call home. What began as a modest storefront with a handful of supplies has grown into a full-service pet shop beloved by the Petville community.

Our shelves are stocked with carefully vetted brands, our live pet area is maintained to the highest welfare standards, and every member of our team shares an unwavering dedication to animal wellbeing. Whether you are welcoming a new puppy, setting up your first aquarium, or simply picking up treats for a longtime companion, we are here to help with expert advice and genuine care.

We do not just sell products — we build relationships. Many of our customers have been with us since day one, and watching their pets grow alongside our store is the greatest reward we could ask for.

Giving Back

Community & Charity

We believe a great pet shop strengthens its community. Here is how PetsMart makes a difference beyond our storefront.

Shelter Partnerships

We partner with local animal shelters to host monthly adoption events and donate supplies to rescue organisations across Petville.

Free Pet Care Workshops

Every quarter we run free workshops on topics like puppy training, aquarium maintenance, and senior pet nutrition for the community.

Charity Donations

A portion of every grooming service and select product sales goes directly to the Petville Animal Welfare Fund supporting vet care for pets in need.

Since 2015, PetsMart has helped over 500 animals find forever homes through our adoption events, donated more than 2,000 pounds of food to local shelters, and funded emergency veterinary care for 120 community pets. We are proud to be more than a shop — we are neighbours who care.
