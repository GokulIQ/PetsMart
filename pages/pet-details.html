About Max

Max is a playful and affectionate Golden Retriever puppy raised in a loving home environment. He is socialised with children and other dogs, crate-trained, and ready to join his forever family.

Personality

Curious, gentle, and eager to please. Max loves fetch, belly rubs, and nap time in sunny spots. He responds well to positive reinforcement and is already learning basic commands like sit and come.

Care Notes

Golden Retrievers need daily exercise and regular grooming. Max should be fed premium puppy food three times daily until six months old. Schedule follow-up vaccinations with your local vet within two weeks of adoption.
