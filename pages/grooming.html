Grooming FAQ

Everything you need to know before your pet's appointment.

Most dogs benefit from a full groom every 4–6 weeks, while cats typically need grooming every 6–8 weeks. Breeds with longer or thicker coats may require more frequent visits. Our groomers can recommend a schedule tailored to your pet.

Yes! We groom both dogs and cats. Our team is trained in feline-friendly handling techniques to keep cats calm and comfortable throughout the session.

Just your pet and any specific instructions! If your pet is on medication or has skin sensitivities, let us know when booking. We provide all shampoos, towels, and grooming tools.

Session length depends on the service and your pet's size and coat type. Nail trims take about 15 minutes, a bath and brush around 45 minutes, and a full groom or spa package can take 90–120 minutes.

For safety and to help pets stay calm, we ask that owners wait in our comfortable lounge area. You are welcome to check in at any time, and we will call you as soon as your pet is ready for pickup.
